ABOUT AMAZON!



Amazon's About page provides a comprehensive overview of the company, emphasizing its mission to be Earth's most customer-centric company where people can find and discover anything they might want to buy online. Founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994 as an online bookstore, Amazon has since diversified into various sectors including e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. The page highlights Amazon's core values of customer obsession, long-term thinking, passion for invention, and operational excellence. It also showcases Amazon's global reach, detailing its operations in numerous countries and its wide range of services and products such as Amazon Prime, AWS, Alexa, Kindle, and its physical retail stores like Amazon Go and Whole Foods Market. Additionally, Amazon's commitment to sustainability, community engagement, and employee welfare is emphasized, along with the company's continuous innovation in technology. The page also provides information about Amazon's leadership team, reflecting the company's dedication to serving its customers, fostering innovation, and making a positive impact on the world.
